## Ingredients

* 3 cups gin
* 1 bay leaf

## Equipment required

* Blender
* Measuring cups

## Method

1. Add gin to blender
2. Pre-blend gin for 10 seconds
3. Add bay leaf
4. Blend for further 20 seconds
5. Serve
